Latitude vs Weather

The equator is 0 degrees latitude, the exact center of the planet earth, this belt is known for warm weather, tropical beaches and deep jungles. But just how does the weather differ from those of more northern latitudes? In this project we will find out with the help of graphs plotting the temperature, humditiy, cloudiness, and wind speed from the equator north.

In each of the links in the dropdown menu you will find each graph and a short description of what each plots along with a short analysis of each. In the data tab you will find a list of the cities plotted on the graphs with their latitudes and longitudes. Finally, in the tab marked comparison you will find all of the graphs displayed together.
